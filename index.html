How to request

Ideally, every student should have a computer available to study at home. But with the sudden switch to remote learning, many students either don't have a device or need to share one with many people. Also, in some locations, internet connectivity is an issue.

If you are a teacher and you or your students need a device to study, or if your internet connection is not sufficient to stream or follow online lectures, please fill the request form

Smart devices for home schooling for those who need them the most.

Even though many students are now officially on distant studying, many still don't have devices they need to study from home.

The initiative "Every Student Online" in collaboration with schools and local governments connects families in need to organizations that have devices they can donate. This way we can make sure the devices reach those who need them the most.
