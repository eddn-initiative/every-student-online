Every Student Online

About Us

Why: to make sure that every pupil receives a high-quality education in the circumstances of forced remote learning.

How: by inviting companies and private households to donate unused laptops and tablets. The devices laying around unused at your place could support children tremendously during the remote learning period.

What: The donated equipment will be distributed to schools and municipalities representatives, who will further distribute it to their students. These measures ensure that the devices are being allocated correctly and reach the pupils in need.

Inspiration

Due to the pandemic, a lot of countries had to switch to remote learning. However, in several areas it resulted in children being left out and unable to follow their lectures properly. We are hoping to support as many pupils as possible to keep their access to high-quality education. Currently the initiative is focusing on Italy. In Italy, 14% of families with a child do not have a tablet or PC.

We are inspired by the successful experiences of similar initiatives in other countries:

Estonia

A pilot project “A computer for every student” which, in the past 3 weeks, shipped more than 1,000 computers.

Lithuania

A where they believe that being in quarantine does not mean being not able to learn.

Belgium

A project which is also bringing together the donors with schools and organizations striving for a better learning experience for their students

And many other news and exciting stories that have been shared around the world.
